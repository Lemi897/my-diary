Privacy Policy

Effective date: 22 May 2026 · Last updated: 22 May 2026
MyDiary is a personal productivity app built for personal use. Your data is yours — we do not sell it, share it, or use it for advertising. This policy explains exactly what data is collected and how it is stored.

👤Who We Are

MyDiary is a personal Progressive Web App (PWA) developed and maintained by Maestro, based in Nairobi, Kenya. The app is hosted on Vercel and uses Supabase as its backend database.

This app is currently in personal/testing phase and is not publicly distributed on app stores.

📦What Data We Collect

MyDiary collects only the data you deliberately enter into the app:

  • Account data — your email address and password (managed by Supabase Auth)
  • Profile data — your name and bio (optional)
  • Habits — habit names, categories, tracking logs, and streaks
  • Tasks — task titles, notes, due dates, priorities, and subtasks
  • Journal entries — your written entries, mood scores, health notes, and tags
  • Goals — goal titles, descriptions, progress, and milestones
  • Finance data — expense descriptions, amounts, categories, debts, and savings goals
  • Streak and XP data — daily activity tracking for gamification

🚫What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts
  • We do not collect contact lists, photos, or files from your device
  • We do not share any data with third parties for marketing purposes

🔒How Your Data Is Protected

All data is stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database hosted on AWS infrastructure in a data centre that complies with SOC 2 Type II standards.

  • Row Level Security (RLS) — every table in the database enforces RLS policies, meaning you can only ever read or write your own data. No other user can access your records.
  • Authentication — all access to the app requires a valid authenticated session via Supabase Auth (JWT tokens). Unauthenticated requests are rejected.
  • HTTPS — all data in transit is encrypted via TLS/HTTPS enforced by Vercel.
  • Passwords — passwords are never stored in plain text. Supabase Auth handles password hashing using bcrypt.
  • No secret keys in frontend — only the publishable anon key is exposed in the client. Secret keys never leave the server.

🤖AI Features

MyDiary uses the Anthropic Claude API for two optional features:

  • AI Health Suggestions (Journal page) — when you click "Get AI Health Suggestion", your health notes text is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a suggestion.
  • AI Spending Analysis (Finance page) — when you click "Analyse My Spending", a summary of your spending categories and amounts is sent to Anthropic's API.
These AI features are strictly opt-in. No data is sent to Anthropic unless you explicitly click the button. The AI responses are not stored permanently unless you save a journal entry. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy.

💾Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally in your browser's localStorage:

  • Your active colour theme preference
  • Budget limits you set in the Finance page
  • Worldcoin (WLD) balance you enter manually
  • Currency exchange rates (cached daily to reduce API calls)
  • Notification schedule state
  • Offline action queue (synced when connection is restored)

This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

🔔Push Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app schedules daily reminders at 7:00am and 8:00pm. These notifications are generated entirely on your device by the service worker — no data is sent to any server to trigger them. You can revoke notification permission at any time in your browser settings.

📱PWA & Service Worker

MyDiary is a Progressive Web App. When installed, it uses a service worker to cache static assets for offline use. The service worker does not track your activity or send any data anywhere.

🗑️Your Rights & Data Deletion

Since this is a personal app, you have full control over your data:

  • Access — all your data is visible directly in the app
  • Edit — you can edit any entry at any time
  • Delete — you can delete individual records from within the app
  • Full deletion — to delete your account and all data, contact the developer or delete your records directly via the Supabase dashboard
  • Export — journal entries can be exported from the Profile page

🍪Cookies

MyDiary does not use tracking cookies. Supabase Auth uses a session cookie to maintain your login state. This is strictly necessary for the app to function and does not track your behaviour.

👶Children's Privacy

MyDiary is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

🔄Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top will be updated. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
